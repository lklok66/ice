Welcome

Welcome to the IceGrid Admin Online Help

IceGrid Admin is the graphical administration tool for IceGrid, the server-deployment and monitoring service for Ice.

These pages describe how to use the IceGrid Admin tool, and assume some familiarity with both Ice and IceGrid.

Getting Started with Ice and IceGrid

Ice and IceGrid are described in detail in the Ice manual.

For a quick overview of IceGrid, please read "Teach Yourself IceGrid in 10 Minutes" in issue 19 of the Connections newsletter.

Need Help?

If you have trouble with IceGrid Admin or any other Ice component, we recommend to browse the Ice user forums and post your questions there.

ZeroC also offers commercial support by e-mail and by phone. Please contact sales@zeroc.com for details.
