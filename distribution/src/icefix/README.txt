======================================================================
IceFIX
======================================================================

IceFIX is a FIX session concentrator that allows multiple client 
applications to share a single FIX session.

IceFIX is available under the terms and conditions of the GNU General
Public License v2 (see the ICE_FIX_LICENSE.txt file).
