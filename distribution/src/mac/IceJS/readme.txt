Installing IceJS 0.1.0

Double-click on IceJS-0.1.0.pkg and follow the instructions to install IceJS on your computer.

Removing IceJS 0.1.0

To remove IceJS 0.1.0 from your computer, execute the uninstall.sh script as root in a Terminal:

  $ cd /Volumes/IceJS-0.1.0
  $ sudo ./uninstall.sh
   
uninstall.sh removes all IceJS 0.1.0 installations from your computer.
